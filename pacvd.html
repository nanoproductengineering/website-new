Plasma-Assisted Chemical Vapor Deposition

NPE has developed a series of large area arc plasma reactors for PACVD processes including deposition of diamond coatings. These reactors have deposition area up to several sq. meters and operate in the pressure range 1 mTorr to 100 Torr. The deposition rate of diamond films in NPE PACVD reactors is ranging from 1 to 30 µm/hr.
